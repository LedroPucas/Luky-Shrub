Lucky Shrub - Transform Your Space into a Green Haven

Welcome to Lucky Shrub! Our website is your gateway to the garden design company located in Tucson, Arizona that brings stunning outdoor spaces to life.
Lucky Shrub offers garden design, creation, maintenance and landscaping services, ensuring each client has a unique and welcoming environment, perfect for relaxing or entertaining.

Additionally, we are a full-service plant nursery with a selection of indoor and outdoor plants.
Whether you want to transform your backyard or decorate a small space, we have everything you need to create the oasis of your dreams.

Lucky Shrub was founded by Jason and Maria, a plant-loving couple. Jason the garden architect
is responsible for creating custom designs and overseeing their implementation. Maria takes care of marketing and management of the nursery,
ensuring our customers receive the best experience possible.

Our goal: To create gardens that are more than just outdoor spaces,
but places that inspire and bring peace to those who visit them.

Explore our website to learn more about our services and how we can transform your space!
